Frequently Asked Questions

What is SCICOMM?

SCICOMM is a final project given to undergraduate biology students enrolled in Environmental Ecology at the University of Dayton. It is assigned at the beginning of the semester and due at the end.

What does SCICOMM stand for or mean?

SCICOMM is an abbreviation of two words: Scientific Communication.
