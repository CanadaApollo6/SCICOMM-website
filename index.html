Welcome to SCICOMM, a catalog of undergraduate
work, communicating science to the world.

This work has been done by students in a combined graduate/undergraduate environmental ecology course at the University of Dayton. These projects aim to take complex scientific topics and make them easy for all to understand. Have a look around! Images from Unsplash and students.

Life in Deserts

A high-level overview of the impacts that climate change is having on deserts.

Project by Riel St. Amand

Chasing Coral

The major impacts of climate change on coral reefs, and why they happen.

Project by Riel St. Amand

Big Sky Country

What does climate change mean for our main food source, grasslands?

Project by Riel St. Amand

Cascadian Climate

How our changing climate affects the towering rainforests of Cascadia.

Project by Riel St. Amand
